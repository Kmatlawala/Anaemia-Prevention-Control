# Animia Health App - Privacy Policy

## Data Collection and Usage

### SMS Permissions
Animia requests permission to send SMS messages for the following healthcare purposes:

1. **Patient Reminders**: Send appointment reminders and follow-up notifications
2. **Health Updates**: Notify patients about test results and health status
3. **Emergency Alerts**: Send critical health information when needed
4. **Care Coordination**: Communicate with healthcare providers and family members

### What We Do NOT Do
- We do NOT read your existing SMS messages
- We do NOT access your SMS history
- We do NOT share your phone number with third parties
- We do NOT send marketing messages

### Data Security
- All patient data is encrypted and stored securely
- SMS messages are sent only to verified patient contacts
- We comply with healthcare data protection regulations
- Access to patient data is restricted to authorized healthcare personnel only

### Your Rights
- You can opt-out of SMS notifications at any time
- You can request deletion of your data
- You can view and update your contact information
- You can contact us with privacy concerns
