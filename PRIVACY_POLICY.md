# Anaemia Shield - Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** [Current Date]

## Introduction
Anaemia Shield ("we," "our," or "app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your health information when you use our healthcare application.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Health Information
- Patient registration data (name, age, gender, date of birth)
- Health screening results (hemoglobin levels, anemia classification)
- Medical history and treatment records
- Contact information (phone number, email address)
- Aadhaar number (hashed for security)
- Location data (for healthcare service delivery)

### Device Information
- Device type and operating system
- App usage statistics
- Network information

## How We Use Your Information

### Healthcare Services
- Patient registration and management
- Health screening and assessment
- Treatment tracking and medication adherence monitoring
- Follow-up scheduling and reminders
- Healthcare reporting and analytics

### SMS Notifications
We use SMS permission (SEND_SMS only) exclusively for:
- Appointment reminders
- Follow-up care notifications
- Health status updates
- Treatment adherence reminders

**We do NOT:**
- Read your SMS messages
- Access your SMS history
- Act as a default SMS handler
- Intercept or receive SMS messages

## Data Security
- All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
- Secure authentication with JWT tokens
- Role-based access control
- Regular security audits
- Compliance with healthcare data protection standards

## Data Sharing
- We do NOT sell your personal information
- We do NOT share data with third parties for marketing
- Data is shared only with authorized healthcare personnel
- We may share anonymized data for public health reporting

## Your Rights
- Access your health data
- Request corrections to your information
- Opt-out of SMS notifications
- Request data deletion
- Export your data

## Children's Privacy
Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

## Data Retention
We retain your health data as required by healthcare regulations and for providing ongoing care services.
