Request Account Deletion - Anaemia Shield

How to delete your account and associated data

Method 1: Delete Account from App

  1. Open the Anaemia Shield app on your device
  2. Navigate to Settings (accessible from the main menu or dashboard)
  3. Scroll down and tap "Delete Account" (red button with delete icon)
  4. Confirm your request:
    • For Admin users: Enter your password to confirm
    • For Patient users: Confirm the deletion in the popup dialog
  5. Your account will be deleted and you will be automatically logged out

Method 2: Contact Us Directly

Email Us

Email: [Your Support Email Address]

Subject: Account Deletion Request

Please include in your email:

  • Your registered phone number or email address
  • Your full name as registered in the app
  • Clear request for account deletion

Response Time: We will process your request within 30 days of receipt.

⚠️ Important: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your personal data will be removed or anonymized.

What Happens After Deletion?

  • ✅ Your account will be permanently deleted
  • ✅ All personal information (name, phone, email, address) will be removed
  • ✅ You will no longer receive notifications or updates
  • ✅ You will be logged out of the app
  • ❌ This action cannot be undone

Important Notes

  • Healthcare Records: If you are a patient, your healthcare provider may retain anonymized medical records as required by healthcare regulations for public health reporting purposes.
  • Admin Accounts: If you are a healthcare worker (admin), please ensure another admin account exists before deleting your account. The last admin account cannot be deleted.
  • Data Anonymization: For patients, personal identifiers are removed but anonymized health data may be retained for public health purposes.

Need Help?

If you have questions about account deletion or need assistance, please contact our support team.
